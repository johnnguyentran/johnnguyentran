Organizations & Clubs

Leaders in IT

I am currently on the eBoard for Leaders in IT with the role of marketing. Leaders in IT work with companies to host networking events and provide opportunities for students to find a career or internship relating to information technology.

Design for America - MSU

In the 2018 fall semester, I became a member of Design for America (DFA) at MSU. Design for America is a national design innovation organization, and it identifies issues within the community along with working with community members to create ideas and concepts that address those challenges. My group and I were able to present our project to Humania in Louisville, Kentucky.

Alternative Spartan Break

In Spring 2018, I joined Alternative Spartan Break on a trip during my spring break to Miami, Florida to learn about human trafficking through the Life of Freedom Center (Sharing One Love) . We spoke to victims and learned about ways to combat human trafficking along with ways to raise awareness about it in our own community.

Spartasoft

Spartasoft is a game development club at MSU and it teaches interested students the basics of game development. It also hosts a game jam every semester, in which members are given a few days to create and develop a video game. I was a part of this club in 2016, and I was able to learn the basics about developing a game.
